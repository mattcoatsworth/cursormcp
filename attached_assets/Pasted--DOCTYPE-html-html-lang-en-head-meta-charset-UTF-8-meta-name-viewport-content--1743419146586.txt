        <li><span class="bold">Virgin Queens Battling for Dominance</span> &ndash; When a new queen emerges in a hive, she will seek out any other developing queen larvae or pupae and sting them to death before they hatch. If two or more virgin queens emerge at the same time, they will <span class="bold">fight to the death</span> using their stingers, as only one can rule the hive.</li>

        <li><span class="bold">Swarming Situations</span> &ndash; When a colony swarms, the old queen leaves with part of the hive to start a new colony, while a new queen takes over the original hive. If multiple new queens emerge, they will battle until only one survives.</li>
